Finding Influencers · The System

How to Find
100 More Yourself

Once you've contacted the 12 targets above, use these methods to continuously find new influencers. You should always have 5–10 new people in your pipeline every week.

🎯
The golden rule: Only contact influencers who post content similar to what Orrisoak is about — luxury skincare, anti-aging, makeup tutorials, beauty routines, or glow/radiance focused content. A beauty influencer who posts about budget drugstore products is NOT a good match for Orrisoak's premium positioning.
01
Hashtag Hunting on Instagram

Search these hashtags on Instagram. Look at top posts and recent posts. Find accounts with 5K–80K followers posting beauty content. Check their engagement — comments and saves matter more than likes.

#LuxurySkincare #AntiAgingSkincare #SkincareRoutine #GlowingSkin #24KSkincare #SkincareObsessed #MakeupTutorial #BeautyOver40
02
Competitor Research

Go to Instagram and search luxury skincare brands similar to Orrisoak — brands like Tatcha, Perricone MD, or Sunday Riley. Look at who is tagging them or posting reviews. Those influencers are ALREADY interested in your category and will be easy to convert.

Search: @tatcha Search: @sunday.riley Search: @drperricone Check Tagged Photos
03
Free Tools to Speed It Up

Use free versions of these tools to find influencers by niche, follower count, and location. Filter for engagement rate above 3%. Export a list and start outreach.

modash.io (free trial) heepsy.com (free) Instagram Search TikTok Search thesocialcat.com
04
What to Check Before Contacting

Before DMing anyone, spend 60 seconds checking these 4 things. If they pass all 4, add them to your outreach list immediately.

✓ Collab/PR email in bio ✓ Last post under 2 weeks ago ✓ Real comments (not bots) ✓ Beauty niche content
05
TikTok Search Strategy

TikTok has massive organic beauty creators. Search these terms in TikTok's search bar. Filter by "Creators." Look for accounts under 100K with 10K–100K likes per video — those are the sweet spot for organic engagement.

"luxury skincare routine" "anti aging serum review" "24k gold skincare" "skincare over 40" "magnetic makeup brush"
06
The Referral Play

After a successful gifting collab, ask your influencer: "Do you know any other beauty creators who might love Orrisoak?" A warm introduction from a fellow creator has a 3–4× higher acceptance rate than a cold DM.

Ask after first post Offer referral gift Build a network
Outreach Messages · Copy & Send

3 Templates.
Proven to Get Replies.

These messages are short, personal, and non-pushy — exactly how luxury brands should reach out. Never copy-paste without personalizing the [BRACKET] parts. Always mention something specific about their content in the first line.

💡
The #1 rule: Your first line must prove you actually looked at their profile. Mention their most recent post, a specific video, or something unique about their bio. Generic openers get ignored. Personalized openers get replies. Spend 30 seconds per influencer on this — it's worth it.
Template 1 · Instagram DM Use for: Nano & Micro · Gifting only
Hi [First Name] 👋 I just watched your [recent post/reel about X] and your skincare content is exactly the kind of genuine, quality content we love. I'm reaching out from Orris & Oak — we make a luxury 24K gold and black truffle skincare line (orrisoak.com). We'd love to send you our complete ritual: the Gold & Truffle Anti-Aging Serum, the Hydrating Glow Cleanser, and our 4-in-1 Magnetic Brush — completely complimentary, no strings attached. If you genuinely love the products, we'd be thrilled if you shared your honest experience. If it's not for you, no worries at all — they're yours to keep. Would you be open to receiving them? 🌟

💬 Why it works: Short, specific, no pressure, clear offer. "No strings attached" removes all friction. Works on Instagram DM or email. Expect 30–50% reply rate when personalized.

Template 2 · Email Outreach Use for: Micro with business email in bio
Subject: Gifting collab? — Orris & Oak luxury skincare for [their handle] Hi [Name], I came across your profile through your recent post on [specific content] — beautiful work, and your audience's engagement really shows how much they trust your recommendations. I'm [your name] from Orris & Oak (orrisoak.com), a luxury skincare brand built around 24K gold and rare black truffle extract. We're reaching out to a small group of creators whose aesthetic and audience feel like a genuine fit — and yours stood out. We'd love to gift you our complete 3-product ritual: ✦ 24K Gold & Black Truffle Anti-Aging Serum ✦ Hydrating Glow Cleanser ✦ 4-in-1 Magnetic Makeup Brush No deliverables required — we genuinely believe you'll love the products. If you'd like to share your experience, we'd be grateful, but there's zero pressure. Would you be interested? Happy to send over more details. Warm regards, [Your Name] Orris & Oak | orrisoak.com | 312-985-6684

📧 Subject line tip: Include their handle in the subject — it increases open rates by 40%. Always sign with your real name and phone number. It builds credibility and shows you're a real brand.

Template 3 · Follow-Up DM Use for: No reply after 5–7 days
Hey [Name]! Just following up on my DM from last week about the Orris & Oak gifting 🌿 I know your inbox gets busy — totally understand! Still happy to send over the Gold Serum + full ritual if you're interested. Lots of creators have been loving the 24K gold texture 💛 No rush at all — just wanted to make sure it didn't get buried! [Your name] @ Orris & Oak

🔁 Send this once, 5–7 days after your first message. Never follow up more than twice — it damages the brand impression. This friendly nudge recovers about 15–20% of non-replies.
