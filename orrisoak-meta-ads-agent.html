Launch Checklist · Before Your First Ad

From Zero to
First Ad Live

Complete every step before spending a single dollar on ads. This prevents wasted budget and account issues.

Step 1 · Meta Business Setup

✓
business.facebook.com
Create a Meta Business Account (free) — go to business.facebook.com and sign up with your Facebook account
✓
Business Manager
Connect your Orrisoak Facebook Page and Instagram account to your Business Manager
✓
Ads Manager
Add your credit card as payment method in Ads Manager → Billing → Payment Methods

Step 2 · Meta Pixel (Critical)

✓
Events Manager → Connect Data Source
Create a Meta Pixel in Events Manager — this tracks who visits orrisoak.com so you can retarget them later
✓
WooCommerce → Facebook for WooCommerce plugin
Install the free "Facebook for WooCommerce" plugin on your site and connect it to your Pixel — this installs the tracking code automatically
✓
Verify with Meta Pixel Helper Chrome extension
Install Meta Pixel Helper (free Chrome extension) and visit orrisoak.com — it should show your Pixel firing green

Step 3 · Launch First Ad

✓
Ad Copy AI tab
Generate your first ad copy using the Ad Copy AI tab — Gold Serum, Awareness campaign, Luxury tone
✓
Creative Briefs tab → Bing Image Creator
Generate your ad image using the Creative Brief prompt in Bing Image Creator — download the best result
✓
Ads Manager → Create Campaign
Create your first campaign: Objective = Awareness, Budget = $5/day, Audience = Women 35-60, Interests = Anti-aging skincare
✓
Review & Publish
Review your ad in the preview — check it looks right on mobile (80% of views are mobile). Hit Publish. Meta reviews within 24 hours.
✓
Wait 7 days before making changes
Do NOT touch the ad for 7 days. Meta's algorithm needs time to optimize. Changing it early resets the learning phase and wastes budget.
