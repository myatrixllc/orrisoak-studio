Campaign Guide · Step by Step

Create Your First
Meta Ad Campaign

Follow each step exactly. Click "Mark Done" when complete to move to the next step. I'll tell you exactly what to click inside Meta Ads Manager.

💡
Your first campaign: Gold Serum · Brand Awareness · $5/day · Women 35–60 · Anti-aging interests. This is the exact campaign to start with. Do not change the product or budget yet.
1
Open Meta Ads Manager
Current

You need to be in Ads Manager — not Business Suite. They look similar but Ads Manager is where you actually create campaigns.

Action
Go to adsmanager.facebook.com or click Ads Manager in the left sidebar of Business Suite. Make sure the account selector in the top left shows orrisoak.


2
Create New Campaign
Pending

You'll create a campaign with the Awareness objective. This tells Meta to show your ad to as many relevant people as possible within your budget.

Exactly What to Click
1. Click the green "+ Create" button in the top left
2. A screen appears asking for your objective
3. Select "Awareness" (first option)
4. Campaign name: type "Orrisoak Gold Serum Awareness"
5. Leave everything else as default
6. Click "Next"
3
Set Your Budget & Schedule
Pending

You're now in the Ad Set level. This is where you set how much to spend and when your ad runs.

Exactly What to Enter
1. Budget type: select "Daily budget"
2. Amount: type $5.00
3. Start date: Today
4. End date: Leave blank (run continuously)
5. Ad set name: "Gold Serum · Women 35-60 · Anti-aging"
4
Set Your Target Audience
Pending

This is the most important step — who sees your ad. Copy these settings exactly.

Audience Settings — Copy Exactly
Locations: United States
Age: 35 – 60
Gender: Women
Detailed Targeting → Add interests:
· Anti-aging skincare
· Luxury beauty
· Skincare routines
· Skin care
· Serum (cosmetics)
· Botox (search this — high intent)
Languages: English (All)


5
Set Ad Placements
Pending

Placements determine where on Meta your ad appears — Facebook feed, Instagram feed, Stories, etc.

What to Select
Select "Advantage+ placements" (recommended/automatic)

This lets Meta automatically show your ad wherever it performs best. For a new campaign with no data yet, this is always the right choice. You can manually choose placements later once you have performance data.
6
Create Your Ad
Pending

Now you're at the Ad level — this is where you add your image and copy. Use the Ad Copy AI tab to generate your copy first, then come back here.

What to Fill In
Identity: Select Orris & Oak Facebook page + Instagram
Ad name: "Gold Serum Awareness V1"
Format: Single image
Image: Upload your Bing-generated product image
Primary text: Paste from Ad Copy AI tab
Headline: Paste from Ad Copy AI tab
Description: Paste from Ad Copy AI tab
CTA button: Select "Shop Now"
Website URL: https://orrisoak.com


7
Review & Publish
Pending

Before you publish, do a final check. Meta will review your ad within 24 hours before it goes live.

Final Checklist Before Publishing
✓ Preview looks good on mobile (tap "Mobile" preview)
✓ Website URL goes to orrisoak.com
✓ Facebook page shows as "Orris & Oak"
✓ Instagram shows as "@orrisoak"
✓ Budget shows $5.00/day
✓ Audience: Women 35-60, United States

If everything looks correct → click "Publish"
Meta will review within 24 hours and notify you by email.
⚠️
Important: Do NOT edit the ad for 7 days after publishing. Meta's algorithm needs time to learn. Touching it early resets the learning phase and wastes your budget.
